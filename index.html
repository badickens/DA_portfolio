Project 1: Investigate The Movie Database

Investigate a dataset which contains information on ~10K movies released between 1960 and 2015. This investigation will focus on the popularity of various genres, directors, actors, and movie revenues. Through the process of data cleaning and EDA, several questions were explored through analysis and data visualizations.

Project 2: Sales Rep Data Analysis

This report will investigate a sales representative and revenue dataset. Analysis includes focus on revenue generated, productivity, and most popular products sold. The analysis explores several questions to gain general business insights. For example, What is the total revenue per year? Which quarter has the highest earnings? Which Sales Reps have the largest % of revenue for each of the regions? What are the top 3 products sold?

Project 3: US Location Data - Phase 1

The exercise of gathering data on US zip codes in order to make a resuable dataset. Information also city, county, state, type of zip code, and it's geolocation. Along with the data for each US state, this includes Washington DC, Puerto Rico, and a few codes of some of the remaining US territories. Phase 1 will focus on the process of gathering, cleaning, and storage into an excel file.
